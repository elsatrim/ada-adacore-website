What is a Sector?

A sector is a group of companies or industries that share similar business activities. Sectors help organize and analyze the economy.

Explore some examples below:

Technology

Companies like Apple, Microsoft, and Google belong to the technology sector. They focus on developing and selling tech products and services.

Finance

Banks, insurance companies, and investment firms are part of the finance sector. They manage money and financial assets.

Healthcare

Hospitals, pharmaceutical companies, and medical device manufacturers make up the healthcare sector. They provide health services and products.

Energy

Oil, gas, and renewable energy companies are in the energy sector. They produce and distribute energy resources.
